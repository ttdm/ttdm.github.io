About

By education, I’m ingineer in image and signal processing and PhD in robotics. My picture in the main page comes from my stay in a french laboratory and has been taken while I was an experiment subject for my colleagues.

I’m now a free software developer because I believe this model to be way more ethical than proprietary softwares. I believe most english readers will be fellow developers so I won’t spend much time on it but it is, as you probably know, way more difficult to make a living by creating free softwares than by creating proprietary ones.

Right now, I’m creating softwares that focuses on domains that I believe should non-profit : politics (in its wide definition, as the organisation of social life) and education. Legally, I work under a french status called “auto-entrepreneur” which gives me plenty of freedom as long as I work alone. I aim to build a non-profit company that creates free softwares for associations and communities.

If you have any project that, in your opinion, fit my description, feel free to contact me.

Outside work, I spend most of my time in the mountains …
