Transition Ecologique des Entreprise : Highlighting state support to encourage ecological actions in SMEs Link to heading

This project was launched within the beta-gouv ecosystem in 2023. I joined as a freelance developer in the spring of 2024, primarily taking charge of the backend and data management aspects of the project.

The goal of the initiative is to support small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in their ecological transition. By showcasing the financial and logistical support provided by the State, we create an appealing entry point that allows us to offer these businesses practical solutions for reducing their environmental impact.

Ethique et Thunes : a web app for an ethical finance cooperative Link to heading

Developed during the incubation of a cooperative focused on ethical wealth management, led by Denis Dupré and supported by INRIA, this React.js-based application enables anyone to independently manage their assets while staying true to their ethical principles.

Démarches-villages.fr : a dematerialization platform for small municipalities Link to heading

Online since 2021 and in use by a municipality (fr), this is a tool created and specifically designed for very small municipalities. Developed using Django, it enables municipalities to fulfill the legal obligation of dematerialization for free, providing a truly adapted online space. Creating an account and configuring it takes only a few minutes, requires no expertise, and results in a dedicated and personalized online space.

Symphonium: a piano learning tool Link to heading

Inspired by Synthesia (proprietary software) and PianoFromAbove (open-source but no longer functional), Symphonium was, at its creation, the only cross-platform and open-source software for piano learning. Symphonium is based on Qt and has been downloaded more than 10,000 times. More informations are available on its dedicated website or on the GitHub repository of the project.
