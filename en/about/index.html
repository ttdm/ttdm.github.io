About

Officialy, I’m ingineer in image and signal processing and PhD in robotics.

In practice, I’m wondering what are the impacts of my actions on the society and if what i’m doing is usefull. I feel that our society is way to inequal and that most of those inequalities are baseless (for exemple, why is intellectual work more valued than manual work ?) and that’s why I try to put my skill into use in diverses software projects that aims at reducing the inequalities and that, for most of them, focus on associations and communities.

Legally, I work under a french status called “auto-entrepreneur” which gives me plenty of freedom as long as I work alone. I aim to build a non-profit company that creates free softwares for associations and communities. This would be an alternative to two model that I don’t appreciate. First, I believe that any business in healthcare, politics (in its largest definition, as the organization of social life) or education, among others, should be non-profit. Second, I believe that licensing is a total non-sense since it’s just adding an artificial lock to make more profit.

If you have any project that, in your opinion, correspond to the type of projects that is previously described (free software about associations and communities), feel free to contact me.

Outside work, I spend most of my time in the mountains …

Attempting to walk in a direction where you get paid for actual work...
